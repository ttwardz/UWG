About UWG

UWG aims to be a resource for writers of all levels of skill and experience. We're passionate about the world of writing, before, during after. UWG focuses on unpublished writers because that's most of us, at least for now. From brainstorming and productivity to writing, editing and everything that comes after, this is the place to explore everything you love about being a writer and everything you want to learn.
